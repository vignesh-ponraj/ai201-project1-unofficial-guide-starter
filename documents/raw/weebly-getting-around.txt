Getting Around Downtown Phoenix

There are a variety of transportation options for students living on or commuting to ASU's Downtown Phoenix campus. The free intercampus shuttles, convenient Valley Metro light rail, or parking structures allow students to take full advantage of public transportation or drive between campuses.

By Madison Spencer

Madison Spencer speaks with Political Science Freshman Haley Wells about her preference of transportation and experiences commuting between Downtown Phoenix and other ASU campuses.
